FAVORITE SERIES


BUSTED



SYNOPSIS

A man named M invites seven people to a murder game party hosted by his boss, known as K. As soon as the game begins, M is shot by an unknown perpetrator, which turns the game into a real crime scene. They soon realize that the murder is related to an operation called "Project D", in which they are also involved in.

DIRECTOR

Jo Hyo Jin

DATE RELEASED AND ENDED

It premiered on May 4, 2018, and concluded on January 22, 2021.


Extraordinary Attorney Woo



SYNOPSIS

Extraordinary Attorney Woo tells the story of Woo Young-woo, a lawyer with autism who is raised by her single father. She grows up with one friend at school, Dong Geu-ra-mi, an oddball girl who protects her from school bullies. She graduates at the top of her law school class at Seoul National University.

DIRECTOR

Moon Ji-won and Yoo In-shik

DATE RELEASED

June 29, 2022

Richie Rich



SYNOPSIS

Richie Rich (Macaulay Culkin) has every toy any little boy could ever want, in addition to an adoring mother (Christine Ebersole) and father (Edward Herrmann). Setting out to solve the mystery, Richie hooks up with a group of kids who prove they just might fill the void in his life, while helping him save his folks.
